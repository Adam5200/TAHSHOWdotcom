The Adam Hendrickson Show

Have you ever thought that if your life and what it was could be a show it would succeed and you could make that show? You say, oh please that all sounds crazy. But that thought has existed inside my head ever since I knew anything. It drove me crazy. But what I was interested in was just the music. I began to learn everything that I could about music in shows, music in movies. That's really just the essence of the show. But I will tell you this: there is one thing. One soundtrack that is above the rest. Though I will admit that I am scared to share The Adam Hendrickson Show, the part where it started was high school, my freshman year. Though I once felt quite immersed in my idea for a show, I can not tell it alone. To assist is a purple suited man, who claims to hold some knowledge, that at the end awaits us. And through this story of pain and joy, love and hate, it will show.
